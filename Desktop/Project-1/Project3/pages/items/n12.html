Erased

Erased, known in Japan as Boku dake ga Inai Machi (僕だけがいない街, lit. The Town Where Only I Am Missing), is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Kei Sanbe. It was serialized in Kadokawa Shoten's Young Ace magazine from June 4, 2012 to March 4, 2016, and is licensed in English by Yen Press.[4][5] An anime adaptation by A-1 Pictures aired on Fuji TV's Noitamina programming block from January 8, 2016 to March 25, 2016, and a live action film was released on March 19, 2016. A live-action drama series was released by Netflix on December 15, 2017.
