Rascal Does Not Dream of a Dreaming Girl

Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai,[a] known in Japan under the general name Seishun Buta Yarō (Japanese: 青春ブタ野郎) and short name Aobuta (青ブタ), is a Japanese light novel series written by Hajime Kamoshida and illustrated by Keeji Mizoguchi. ASCII Media Works have published nine volumes since April 2014 under their Dengeki Bunko imprint. A manga adaptation with art by Tsugumi Nanamiya has been serialized in ASCII Media Works' seinen manga magazine Dengeki G's Comic since January 2016. An anime television series adaptation by CloverWorks premiered from October 4 to December 27, 2018. An anime film adaptation titled Rascal Does Not Dream of a Dreaming Girl premiered on June 15, 2019.
