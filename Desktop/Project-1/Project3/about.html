What is an anime?

Anime - this art of animation originated in the XX century. in the East, in distant Japan. But surprisingly, this genre owes its name to a non-Japanese word. As you may have guessed, the word "anime" is just an abbreviation for the English "animation", which has been successfully disseminated because of its ease of pronunciation.
